Bunny Chow Recipe

Home Melktert Bunny Chow Koeksisters

Description

Bunny Chow is a popular South African street food that consists of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with curry. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients

For the Curry:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Serving:

  • 1 large loaf of bread, unsliced
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Make the Curry:
    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
    3. Add the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using), and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
    4. Add the diced tomatoes, chickpeas, and coconut milk, and bring to a simmer.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and let the curry simmer for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  2. Prepare the Bread:
    1. Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise, and hollow out the center to create a bread bowl.
    2. Fill the bread bowl with the curry, and top with fresh coriander leaves.
    3. Serve the bunny chow with lime wedges on the side.
